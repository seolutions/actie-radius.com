The Spa at Hotel Namu is renowned for its opulence and exceptional service. Guests can also enjoy access to the hotel’s other luxurious amenities, making it a perfect destination for a day of indulgence.

Luxe Massage Retreat is another gem in Gangnam's crown. Known for its tranquil ambiance and top-notch treatments, this parlor offers a haven of peace in the city's heart. The Garden Spa stands out for its beautiful, nature-inspired interiors. This eco-luxury spa offers treatments that focus on holistic well-being, using organic and natural products. The "Garden Bliss" package, featuring a combination of massages and body treatments, is designed to detoxify and revitalize.
